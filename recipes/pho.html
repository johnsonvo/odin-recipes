Pho

Description

Ingredients

  • 4 LBS beef soup bones
  • 1 medium onion
  • 4 quarts water
  • 1 (8oz) package dried rice noodles
  • 1 LB beef top sirloin
  • 1.5 cups bean sprouts

Steps

  1. Gather all Ingredients
  2. Preheat
  3. Preheat oven to 425 degrees
  4. Roast beef bones and onion
  5. Transfer beef bones and onion to pot. Bring to boil, then let simmer.
  6. Cook rice noodles; enjoy!
